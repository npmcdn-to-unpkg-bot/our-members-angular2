Creating your User Account and Login

Here is where you create your organisation's account in iBlong2. An email confirming your organisation's login details will be sent to your nominated email address. Once you receive this and activate your account it will be ready to start uploading or entering your member's and other details.

If we can be of any assistance to you during the trial then please don't hesitate to contact us. It is really important for us that you are happy with iBlong2 and that  you are satisfied that it does the job you need of it.

At the end of the 3-month trial we hope you will join us and subscribe. Should you decide however that iBlong2 is not for you then you will be able to delete your account and all data that you have uploaded.

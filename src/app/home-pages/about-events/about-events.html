Organise Events and Group Activities

Organising events and activities associated with your organisation is a job that requires both good organisational skills and great communication with your members.

Whether its a sporting fixture, a formal dinner, an art exhibition, a members meeting or a get together of one of your membership interest groups, iBlong2 can manage member's acceptances and attendances for you.

  • You set up your event in iBlong2, select the members or groups of members you want to invite and click a button and iBlong2 will either email out or print an invitation.
  • If members have email accounts and Internet access they can indicate their attendance and any other event related queries you may need them to answer.
  • It even helps with creating name tags!
  • You can produce reports showing who is attending, if they have any comments such as dietary restrictions and whether they are bringing a guest if that option is offered.

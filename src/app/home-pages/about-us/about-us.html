About Us -behind the people behind iBlong2

Greetings. We are a husband and wife team living in Australia.

When we retired from the workforce we knew we couldn't sit around doing nothing. We had years of experience working in businesses as varied as computer programming, help desk management, computer maintenance, publishing, advertising, bee keeping and event management. It just seemed a waste to not try to use our skills and experience while doing something that we both enjoyed - software programming.

<%--

We had also worked in voluntary capacities on various committees both as treasurer and president so we knew how frustrating it can be to handle the day to day operations of an active group of people who all have a common interest but very little time to spare.

--%>

We designed and built iBlong2 to fill a gap that we believed existed for low cost, easy to use and comprehensive software to help those who serve on committees or boards of any organisation which has members and which needs to manage a membership list, member communications, finances and event co-ordination. We also wanted our software to make it easier to hand over all these tasks to new committees when they take over these roles.

We know that not everyone has the time to learn complicated software - or the inclination! Every aspect of iBlong2 has been designed to be as simple and intuitive as possible. If you find something difficult to understand then we definitely want to know about it!

We have recorded dozens of videos which are integrated into the system and which show you just how easy it is to perform iBlong2 functions. In addition we have written a comprehensive iBlong2 User Guide which is freely available for download so you can use it as a handy reference tool.

We both worked long and hard to bring this software to life and we are very grateful to you for taking the time to view our site, read about us and iBlong2 and hopefully to give us a try.

Help is always close at hand
It is important to us that you find iBlong2 easy to use and perfect for your requirements. We have provided informative video's and printable PDF's for all the major functions within the application. Furthermore there is a comprehensive User Guide which you can download and keep close at hand for reference.

If there is any part of using iBlong2 that you think could work better then please let us know!

Communicate with your members

With iBlong2 you can communicate with all your members through email or post...or you can communicate with just some of them.

  • Perhaps you have some specialised interest groups within your members? You can create a group containing these members and then direct your communication just to them.
  • Keep track of what communications you have sent out and to whom and when they have been sent.
  • Create good looking messages that you can either email with attachments or if your member does not use email then print both them and an address label.
  • Send out subscription reminders to those who have or who are about to fall due.
  • Send out meeting reminders to all members or to groups of your members.
  • Emails from members replying to your email will be forwarded to your nominated email account.

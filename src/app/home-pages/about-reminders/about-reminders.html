Simplify Membership Reminders

Sending your members advice that subscriptions are due can be a time consuming and tedious task.

Sending reminders to collect late payments is even more frustrating. Let iBlong2 show you how to:

  • Easily access a list of all members who fall due by a chosen date and send subscription reminders to those members simply and quickly.
  • If members don't use email - no problem. iBlong2 will print out a subscription reminder - it will even prepare a post label
  • Set the subscription due date based on either a common start/end date or on the date each member joined.
  • Set up any number of membership types - junior, ordinary, concession, senior, life for example.
Easy Payment Receipts and Statements
  • Once a member has paid their membership then iBlong2 can generate and send or print them a receipt with the click of a button - no more tedious hand writing up receipt books.
  • Produce and track receipts for anything your association collects money for - merchandise, donations anything.
  • Run membership reports showing due dates and other details for all members or groups within your membership.
  • Automatically update a members paid-to date when a membership payment is received.
  • Manage part-paid memberships. It will be easy to see which of your members has outstanding membership fees and you will be able to generate membership statements for any member who has paid fees.

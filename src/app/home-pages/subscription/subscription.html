How iBlong2 Subscriptions Work

Subscription to iBlong2 depends on the number of members within the organisation. After much thought it seemed to us that the fairest model is to count only active members. Lapsed members are therefore not counted even though their details are still stored in your iBlong2 records.

Prices work out at less than 50 cents per member pa.

We wanted to make iBlong2 affordable for even the smallest organisation so our rates are constructed accordingly. If your organisation grows and moves into the next price bracket within your subscription year we will simply wait and adjust your following year's membership level accordingly when you renew.

$49* pa for up to 100 active members

This is our Base Level Membership. You can try iBlong2 obligation free for up to 3-months. It gives you full access to all features for up to 100 active members.

$99* pa for 101 to 200 active members

This is our Level 2 Membership. You can try iBlong2 obligation free for up to 3-months. After that time the subscription rate is $99 per annum.

$245* pa for 201 to 500 active members

This is our Level 3 Membership. You can try iBlong2 obligation free for up to 3-months. After that time the subscription rate is $245 per annum.

$480* pa for 501-1000 active members

This is our Level 4 Membership. You can try iBlong2 obligation free for up to 3-months. After that time the subscription rate is $480 per annum.

For numbers of active members over 1000, please contact us.

* all amounts are in $AU.

Track and Record Financial Transactions

The Treasurer's role can be a real drain on your time. With iBlong2 the built in Financial Reporting module makes managing of your association's finances as hassle free and accurate as can be.

  • Create bank deposit lists of all recent checks and cash ready to take to the bank.
  • Reconcile your bank statements to both your income received and expenses paid.
  • Issue tax receipts and record purchases which are then tracked ready to be reconciled against your bank statement.
  • Download your bank statements and import them directly into your iBlong2 finances.
  • Produce a Profit and Loss statement and other account expenditure or income reports for your Committee or Board.
Easy tracking of part-paid memberships
  • iBlong2 will automatically create a membership statement for all members showing any outstanding balances. These can be easily then either printed out or emailed as reminder notices.
  • You will be able to produce Outstanding Membership Fee reports showing all members with outstanding balances.
Accurate Audit Trails
  • iBlong2 keeps track of all changes made to your society or association's membership or financial records along with the time, date and user name.
Automatic Subscription Date Updates
  • iBlong2 will automatically update each members subscription period once you acknowledge receipt of their payment. No more two step processing of memberships!
Easy, no-hassle hand overs

Handing over to the new committee at the end of the season is easy. Simply use the User Administration tool to reallocate your role in iBlong2 to the new committee member.

No more explaining messy spreadsheets or bits of paper. Your handover will be smooth easy and professional.
